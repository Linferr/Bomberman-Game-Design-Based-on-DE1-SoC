Rules

    GAME: This project aims to create a player-versus-player bomb competition game. Prior to the start of the game, the DE1-SoC will display a lobby code, which players can enter on the mobile app to join the session for that particular DE1-SoC. The play area is composed of a 15x15 grid of square tiles. Player characters take up positions on single tiles and can move between tiles during the game. Players compete by laying bombs on the tile they are currently on. Once placed on a tile, a bomb will explode after a short duration, damaging players caught in the explosion radius. Different types of bombs will have different explosion patterns (for instance, circular and cross patterns), creating a tactical gameplay element where players must select the bomb that is best for their situation. Each player can only have one bomb on the field at a time, and can only place the next bomb after their previous one detonates. Players start with a certain amount of 'health points' and lose one every time they are caught in a detonation. Players who lose all their health points are knocked out of the round. The game is over once there is only one player remaining, who is declared the winner.
