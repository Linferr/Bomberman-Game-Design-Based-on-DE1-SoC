Rules

Players

2 players minimum. Support 4 players maximum.

Read More

Playing area

The play area is composed of a 15x15 grid of square tiles.

Read More

Play method

Players compete by laying bombs on the tile they are currently on. Once placed on a tile, a bomb will explode after a short duration, damaging players caught in the explosion radius.

Read More

Winner

Players who lose all their health points are knocked out of the round. The game is over once there is only one player remaining, who is declared the winner.
